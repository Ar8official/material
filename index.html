ISMAIL ADEBAYO is my name, 21years old Nigerian, from a family of 5. Being the second, I have taken it upon myself to see life differently. Growing up with the normal belief that a responsible child must go to school, get a good grade and look for Job literally meaning to work for the government.I've found it weird but maybe I'm the wierd one here, I decided not to give-in to that trending idea. I want to set a life that my wife and children would like in the nearest future, a life I want with my parents but never had as a result of my parents leaving house for work everyday as early as possible and coming late in the dark hours because it what the job demands . Reason I become a freelancer and opted for Digital Marketing, today I'm a Google certified digital marketer specialized in Email Marketing despite having a degree in Engineering from a prestigious university here in Nigeria. I owe that to satisfying my parents, so far it doesn't stop my ambition as a Freelancer.
